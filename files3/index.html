Ramalinga Swamigal
(1823 – 1874)

Light of Grace — Arut Perum Jothi

Ramalinga Swamigal, reverently known as Vallalar, was a Tamil saint-poet and spiritual master born in Marudhur, Tamil Nadu in 1823. He is celebrated as one of the greatest Tamil saints, whose teachings of Universal Compassion (Jeeva Karunyam) and the path of Divine Grace Light transcend all barriers of caste, creed, and religion.

Vallalar's spiritual journey led him to Vadalur and later Mettukuppam, where he established the Samarasa Sutha Sanmarga Sathya Sangam — an institution dedicated to universal brotherhood, feeding the hungry, and attaining divine grace. His hymns, collected in the Thiruvarutpa, are luminous verses that continue to guide seekers on the path of light and love.

The Origin of Seer Karuneegar Sangam

The Seer Karuneegar Madalaya Tharuma Paribalana Sangam was founded in Tiruvannamalai by a group of devoted followers of Vallalar's teachings, inspired by the sacred energy of Annamalaiyar temple and the eternal flame of Arunachala.

The Sangam was established with a singular mission: to uphold the teachings of Ramalinga Swamigal by practising Jeeva Karunyam (compassion toward all living beings), providing food to the needy, conducting spiritual discourses, and maintaining the sanctity of the Madalaya (community hall) for devotees to gather and pray.

Over the decades, the Sangam has grown into a respected spiritual institution in Tiruvannamalai, organising annual Vallalar Jayanthi celebrations, Annadhanam (free food distribution), and cultural programmes celebrating Tamil spirituality and literature.

Vallalar's Teachings

The Eternal Wisdom of Ramalinga Swamigal

✨
🌟

Arut Perum Jothi

Vallalar revealed the path of the Supreme Divine Light of Grace — a luminous consciousness accessible to all souls who seek with sincerity, irrespective of birth or station.

🤲

Jeeva Karunyam

Compassion toward all living beings is the highest spiritual practice. Vallalar taught that seeing hunger in another's eyes is seeing God, and feeding the hungry is the truest worship.

🕊️

Universal Brotherhood

Vallalar's Sanmarga (right path) transcends caste, religion and region. He proclaimed that the one true family is the family of all souls united in divine love.

📖

Thiruvarutpa

His six-volume collection of hymns, the Thiruvarutpa, is a spiritual treasure of Tamil literature — verses that are prayers, philosophical treatises and songs of divine longing, all at once.

🧘

Deathless Body

Vallalar spoke of transforming the physical body through divine grace into an immortal, luminous form — a radical spiritual vision unique in the history of Tamil Saiva Siddhanta.

🔥

Inner Flame

Like the eternal flame at Tiruvannamalai, Vallalar taught seekers to kindle the inner light of knowledge and compassion, letting it burn away ego and illuminate the path to liberation.
